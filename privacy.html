Privacy Policy

Welcome to Bedtime Whispers!

This privacy policy outlines how information collected during the use of the Bedtime Whispers application (hereinafter referred to as the "app") is used and protected. By using this application, you agree to the following policy terms.

1. Collected Information:

  • Personal Information: Information voluntarily provided by users may include name, email address, age, location information, etc. This information may be used to enhance user experience, personalize stories, and fulfill user requests.
  • Device Information: Certain information about your device may be collected while using the app. This may include the device's operating system, model, language settings, IP address, device ID, advertising ID, and similar technical details.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use the application may be collected. This includes which stories you read, your interactions within the app, click history, and similar data.

    • 2. Purpose of Use:

  • We use the collected information to improve user experience, personalize the app, and fulfill user requests.
  • We analyze collected data to evaluate app performance and enhance our services.

    • 3. Third-Party Sharing:

  • User information is not shared or sold to third parties without the explicit consent of the user. However, when sharing stories, you may be subject to the privacy policy of the platform you choose.

    • 4. Security:

  • We take necessary technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of user information. However, we cannot guarantee the complete security of any information transmission or storage.

    • 5. Children's Privacy:

  • While our app targets children, we pay special attention to children's privacy in our information collection processes. We do not collect personal information from children under 13, and we consider age groups when generating stories.

    • 6. User Rights:

  • Users may request the correction, updating, or deletion of provided information. Additionally, if you have any questions or concerns about our information collection processes, please feel free to contact us.
